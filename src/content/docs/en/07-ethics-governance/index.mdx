An AI product can work technically and still cause harm. Hallucinations, bias, privacy violations — as a PM, you're responsible for making your product not just useful, but safe.

## What you'll learn

- What Responsible AI means in practice (not just as a buzzword)
- How guardrails work and when you need them
- Which privacy questions AI products raise
- How to handle hallucinations — in the product and in communication

The next level beyond chatbots: AI systems that execute tasks independently, use tools, and process multi-step workflows. As a PM, you need to understand which autonomy levels exist and where the risks lie.

## What you'll learn

- How multi-agent systems work and when they make sense
- What Tool Use and MCP mean — and why they extend AI capabilities
- Which autonomy levels exist (suggestion, execution, autonomous)
- When a human needs to stay in the loop

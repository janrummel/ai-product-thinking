An AI PRD looks different from a classic PRD. Timelines are less certain, cross-functional collaboration is more complex, and "done" is a moving target. This chapter gives you the tools for the AI product day-to-day.

## What you'll learn

- How to write AI-specific PRDs (eval criteria, data needs, model requirements)
- What the AI product lifecycle looks like (different from traditional software)
- How to collaborate with Data Scientists and ML Engineers
- What Data Quality means for AI products
- How to adapt Agile for AI projects

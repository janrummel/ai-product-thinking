Before you can shape AI products, you need to understand what AI can do — and what it cannot.

This chapter won't give you a computer science degree. It gives you the mental models to make informed product decisions.

## What you'll learn

- How Large Language Models think (and why "think" is the wrong word)
- Where Machine Learning ends and Deep Learning begins
- Why AI products work fundamentally differently from deterministic software
- What Foundation Models are and why they changed the rules

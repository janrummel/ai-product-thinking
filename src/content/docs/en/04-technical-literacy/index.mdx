You don't need to train a model. But you need to understand what your engineering team means when they talk about prompting, RAG, or fine-tuning — and the tradeoffs behind every technical decision.

## What you'll learn

- Prompt Engineering: How to tell a model what you want
- RAG: How to give a model knowledge it doesn't have
- Fine-Tuning: When it's worth customizing a model
- Model Selection: Which model for which purpose
- Cost/Quality Tradeoffs: The economics behind AI products

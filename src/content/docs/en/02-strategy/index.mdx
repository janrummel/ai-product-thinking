Not every problem needs AI. And not every AI feature makes a product better.

This chapter gives you the tools to decide: Where does AI truly pay off? What to build, what to buy? And what happens when AI threatens your existing product-market fit?

## What you'll learn

- When AI is the right solution — and when a simple rule suffices
- Build vs. Buy vs. Blend: using APIs, RAG, fine-tuning, or training your own model
- How AI can destroy existing product-market fit (and what to do about it)
- Where AI creates the most value and how to identify opportunities
